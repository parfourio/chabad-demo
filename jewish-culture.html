Jewish Culture

Celebrating Jewish art, history, food, film, and community life — right here in the Sonoma Valley.

Our Mission

Where Heritage
Comes Alive.

Jewish culture is more than tradition — it's a living, breathing tapestry of food, music, art, film, and community. At Sonoma Valley Chabad, we bring that culture off the page and into the room.

From pop-up delis and cycling clubs to film screenings and VR experiences, our Jewish Culture programming is designed for everyone — deeply observant or curious for the first time.

What We Offer

Cultural Programs

Each program is thoughtfully designed to spark connection, curiosity, and pride in Jewish heritage — in the heart of Wine Country.

Food & Community
Pop-Up NY Jewish Deli
Authentic New York-style Jewish deli right here in Sonoma. Enjoy house-made pastrami, matzo ball soup, latkes, and more — straight from the traditions of the Lower East Side. A taste of home for anyone who's ever loved a great deli.
Seasonal Events Learn More →
Film & Arts
Jewish Cinema
Curated screenings of award-winning Jewish films — from Israeli cinema to Holocaust remembrance documentaries to contemporary Jewish stories from around the world. Followed by community discussion and refreshments.
Monthly Screenings Learn More →
History & Memory
VR Tour of Auschwitz
An immersive virtual reality experience that takes visitors inside Auschwitz-Birkenau. Developed in partnership with Holocaust education organizations, this guided tour ensures the memory of the six million is never forgotten.
By Appointment Learn More →
Outdoor · Shabbat
Shabbat Under the Stars
An outdoor Shabbat experience set against the breathtaking backdrop of Sonoma wine country. Candle lighting, kiddush, and a communal meal under the open sky — a uniquely California expression of Jewish tradition.
Summer Series Learn More →
Active Community
Chabad Cycling Club
Explore the scenic roads and vineyards of Sonoma Valley with fellow members of the community. All skill levels welcome — from casual weekend riders to serious cyclists. Jewish conversation, fresh air, and great company guaranteed.
Weekly Rides Learn More →
Speaker Series
Speaker Series
Hear from remarkable guests including diplomats, authors, and Jewish community leaders. Past speakers have included Israel's Spokesperson to the United Nations. These evenings bring the wider Jewish world to Sonoma's doorstep.

Why It Matters

Culture Is How We
Pass It On.

Jewish culture isn't separate from Jewish life — it is Jewish life. The stories we tell, the food we share, the art we make, the history we carry — these are the threads that connect every generation.

In Sonoma Valley, we're building a community where those threads are celebrated, explored, and handed down with joy.
