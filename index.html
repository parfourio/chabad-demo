About Us

A Place That
Feels Like Home.

Chabad Jewish Center of Sonoma Valley is dedicated to providing an open-door environment for strengthening and enhancing Jewish values and identity — for every person, regardless of background, philosophy, or level of commitment.

Whether you're looking to connect with your heritage, celebrate Shabbat with community, enroll your children in Jewish education, or simply explore — you belong here. No affiliation required, no expectations attached.

Rabbi Mendy Wenger leads the Sonoma Valley Chabad Jewish Center with warmth, wisdom, and a deep commitment to every member of the community. Whether you're a lifelong observer or completely new to Jewish life, Rabbi Mendy's door is always open.
